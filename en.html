About us

Why you should choose Blue Limo for the next business travel

Our company was founded with the aim of satisfying all the needs and wishes of our clients. Our team consists of professional drivers with many years of experience and impeccable road knowledge. All our drivers are highly trained, friendly and discreet, with the aim of making your trip a safe and pleasant experience.

Blue Limo believes that every ride should be extraordinary. That is why commitment to the quality of services is reflected in every detail. We adapt to your needs and requirements, whether it's a business trip, airport transfer, wedding or special occasion.

We look forward to the opportunity to provide you with the ultimate driving experience and to show you why we are the first and right choice.\ Contact us today to help you organize your next ride.

Our Services

AIRPORT TRANSFERS

Our airport transfers are carefully designed to provide you with an efficient and comfortable ride. Our drivers follow your flights and adapt to schedule changes, so you can be sure that we will take you to the airport punctually and stress-free or meet you upon landing.

BUSINESS TRIPS

Provide yourself with a flawless business trip with our vehicles. Our drivers are trained to provide a discreet service and to take you safely to your meeting, conference or any other business destination.

WEDDINGS

On your special day, we'll make sure you have a glamorous ride. Our fleet of limousines will enable you to arrive in style and elegance.

TOURIST TOURS

Get to know the city or region in a unique way with us. All our drivers are familiar with the most interesting locations and sights and will gladly take you through the most beautiful destinations of your choice.

SPECIAL OCCASIONS

Regardless of whether it is a birthday, anniversary or any other special occasion, our limo service will complete your celebration. Enjoy elegant transportation and an unforgettable experience.
